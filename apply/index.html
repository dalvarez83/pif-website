PIF Application Overview

  • Who may apply: All United States citizens and nationals (residents of American Samoa and Swains Islands) are eligible to apply. Non-U.S. citizens, current federal employees, and GSA contractors are not eligible to apply.

  • Applications due: April 30, 2021, 11:59 PM Pacific Time

  • How to apply: Complete our application form, following all instructions.

  • Salary: This role’s annual salary is $144,128. This is a GS-15 Step 1 role (the GS system defines many federal salaries - learn more about what this means here).

  • Benefits: All PIFs receive federal employee benefits. This includes medical, vision, and dental insurance, FSA accounts, life insurance, paid leave, and the Thrift Savings Plan (the government version of a 401K) with up to five percent matching.

  • Location: While our program is Washington, D.C.-based, the vast majority of our staff will continue working remotely until it is safe to return to in-person work. Some staff are living outside of D.C. right now and have onboarded remotely with the understanding that they will move to D.C. once in-person work resumes.

Preparing to apply

Here is everything you’ll need to submit your PIF application.

  • Select your track: In our application, we ask every candidate to select one of five tracks (data and AI; product; design and experience; digital strategy; or software and infrastructure). The track you select should best align with your area of expertise. It’s okay if you have expertise in more than one area! We ask that you select whichever most closely matches your skill set. Doing so will help our team thoughtfully pair candidates with projects. Track descriptions are below.

    Additionally, the track you chose will not limit the projects that you can qualify for. The PIF team will match all qualified candidates to the appropriate projects as many projects require multi-disciplinary backgrounds.

  • Before you apply: Read through the minimum qualifications for your track of choice, which are linked in “Selecting a PIF track” below. Only applicants whose resumes reflect at least one year’s experience of the minimum qualifications listed in the job posting will be considered.

  • Resume: Federal hiring requires that your resume must explicitly reflect the minimum qualifications cited in the job posting. More information regarding resume best practices can be found here. Detail is encouraged!

  • Statement sharing why you want to join PIF (no more than 900 characters)

  • Optional: Links to professional portfolios or profiles (i.e. your LinkedIn, GitHub, etc.)

Our interview process: What to expect!

For candidates selected to interview, here’s our process. You will receive confirmation of your application. We are not able to provide updates on the status of your application until all fellows have been selected.

  • First round interviews include three conversations: skills (your area of expertise), core competencies (behavioral questions, leadership, and navigating ambiguity), and core values (including diversity, equity, and inclusion).

  • Second round (and final) interviews are with PIF’s federal agency partner(s). This is a conversation with the agency’s PIF project champion. The PIF team will share finalist resumes with agencies who then identify candidates that they would like to interview.

  • Format: All interviews occur virtually and are typically scheduled for an hour in length. We offer live captioning or other accessibility assistance to all candidates. If you are selected to interview, a member of our Talent Team will be in touch to ensure we’ll have any accessibility considerations covered.

  • Offer Process: Once the agency interviews are complete, the PIF team will meet with each agency to discuss their selections. This gives us our list of finalists to whom we make offers! We operate within the U.S. General Services Administration (GSA), and the GSA HR team will then make tentative offers to selected candidates. The PIF team will also set up calls with each incoming PIF to welcome them into our program and community, and answer any questions!
